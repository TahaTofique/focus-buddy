case_study.md — SentinAI
PROBLEM

Most systems generate constant telemetry, but nobody's watching it until something already broke.

APPROACH

Built a live monitoring engine on top of psutil, feeding real machine metrics into anomaly-detection models (Isolation Forest, alongside Random Forest and Linear Regression baselines), surfaced through a Streamlit dashboard.

KEY DECISION

Chose Isolation Forest over supervised models because it doesn't need labeled failure data — most real systems never have a labeled "this was a failure" dataset to train on.

OUTCOME

A working anomaly-flagging system, fully reviewed and defended as a university project — including a full technical walkthrough and viva.
